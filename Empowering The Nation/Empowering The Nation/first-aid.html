First Aid Course

Learn life-saving skills to respond confidently in emergencies.

Course Overview

The First Aid course aims to provide learners with essential first aid awareness and basic life support skills. It prepares participants to handle emergencies confidently and respond effectively in critical situations.

Course Details

  • Duration: 6 Months
  • Mode: In-person Training
  • Certificate: Nationally recognized

Course Purpose

Purpose: To provide first aid awareness and basic life support.

Course Content

  • Wounds and bleeding
  • Burns and fractures
  • Emergency scene management
  • Cardio-Pulmonary Resuscitation (CPR)
  • Respiratory distress (e.g. choking, blocked airway)

Fees

Course Fee: R1,500 (includes training materials and certification)
