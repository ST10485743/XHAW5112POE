Child Minding Course

Gain the skills to care for children safely, responsibly, and professionally.

Course Overview

This course equips learners with essential skills to provide safe and responsible child care. Students learn about child development, health, hygiene, and creating stimulating environments for young children.

Course Details

  • Duration: 6 Months
  • Mode: In-person Training
  • Certificate: Nationally recognized

Course Purpose

Purpose: To equip learners with the ability to care for infants and toddlers in a home or daycare environment while ensuring their safety and well-being.

Course Content

  • Understanding child growth and development
  • Creating a safe and stimulating environment
  • Health, nutrition, and hygiene for children
  • Play-based learning activities
  • Emergency procedures and first aid basics

Fees

Course Fee: R1,500 (includes training materials and certification)
