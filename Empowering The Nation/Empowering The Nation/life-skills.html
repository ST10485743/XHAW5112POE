Life Skills Course

Empowering individuals with the knowledge and confidence to navigate life successfully.

Course Overview

This six-month course helps learners build essential personal and professional skills such as communication, financial literacy, problem-solving, and teamwork.

Course Details

  • Duration: 6 Months
  • Mode: In-person Training
  • Certificate: Nationally recognized

Course Purpose

Purpose: To provide learners with essential skills to navigate basic life necessities.

Course Content

  • Opening a bank account
  • Basic labour law (know your rights)
  • Basic reading and writing literacy
  • Basic numeric literacy

Fees

Course Fee: R1,500 (includes training materials and certification)
