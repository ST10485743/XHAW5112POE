Sewing Course

Master tailoring and design for your own business or creative passion.

Course Overview

Gain hands-on experience in stitching, tailoring, and basic clothing design. This course prepares learners to start small businesses or create their own fashion lines.

Course Details

  • Duration: 6 Months
  • Mode: In-person Training
  • Certificate: Nationally recognized

Course Purpose

Purpose: To provide alterations and new garmet tailoring services .

Course Content

  • Types of stiches
  • Threading a sewing machine
  • Sewing buttons, zips, hems and seams
  • Alterations
  • Designing and sewing new garments

Fees

Course Fee: R1,500 (includes training materials and certification)
