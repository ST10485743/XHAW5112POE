Cooking Course

Learn practical cooking skills for delicious and nutritious meals.

Course Overview

Learn how to prepare delicious, healthy, and affordable meals in this six-week hands-on cooking course. Ideal for domestic workers and aspiring caterers.

Course Details

  • Duration: 6 Weeks
  • Mode: In-person Training
  • Certificate: Nationally recognized

Course Purpose

Purpose: To prepare and cook nutritious family meals.

Course Content

  • Nutritional requirements for a healthy body
  • Types of protein, carbohydrates and vegetables
  • Planning meals
  • Tasty and nutritious recipes
  • Preparation and cooking of meals

Fees

Course Fee: R750 (includes training materials and certification)
