Landscaping Course

Design, create, and maintain beautiful outdoor spaces with professional skills.

Course Overview

This practical course covers garden design, plant selection, lawn maintenance, and outdoor space beautification to prepare students for landscaping jobs or self-employment.

Course Details

  • Duration: 6 Months
  • Mode: In-person Training
  • Certificate: Nationally recognized

Course Purpose

Purpose: To provide landscaping services for new and established gardens.

Course Content

  • Indigenous and exotic plants and trees
  • Fixed sructures (fountains, statues, benches, tables, built-in-braai)
  • Balancing of plants and trees in a garden
  • Aesthetics of plants shapes and colours
  • Garden layout

Fees

Course Fee: R1,500 (includes training materials and certification)
