Garden Maintenance Course

Learn practical garden care, lawn maintenance, and basic landscaping skills.

Course Overview

This six-week course provides practical training in garden care, plant pruning, lawn care, and basic landscaping maintenance for homes and small estates.

Course Details

  • Duration: 6 Weeks
  • Mode: In-person Training
  • Certificate: Nationally recognized

Course Purpose

Purpose: To provide basic knowledge of watering, pruning and planning in a domestic garden.

Course Content

  • Water restrictions and the watering of indigenous and exotic plants
  • Pruning and propagation of plants
  • Planting techniques for different plant types

Fees

Course Fee: R750 (includes training materials and certification)
